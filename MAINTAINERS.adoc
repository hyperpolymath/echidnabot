== Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for:

* Reviewing and merging pull requests
* Triaging issues and feature requests
* Ensuring code quality, security, and documentation standards
* Managing releases, tags, and versioning
* Upholding the
  link:CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md[Code of Conduct]
* Coordinating with sibling bots in the
  https://github.com/hyperpolymath/gitbot-fleet[gitbot-fleet]
* Keeping `.machine_readable/6a2/STATE.a2ml` in sync with reality

== Becoming a Maintainer

Contributors who demonstrate:

* Consistent, high-quality contributions
* Understanding of the project's goals, the estate's language policy,
  and the Tier-1 Verifier role within the fleet
* Constructive participation in discussions and reviews
* Commitment to the project's long-term health

…may be invited to become maintainers at the discretion of existing
maintainers.

== Decision Making

* **Routine decisions** (bug fixes, minor improvements, documentation,
  dependency bumps) can be made by any maintainer.
* **Significant changes** (new public surface, new bot mode, new
  prover integration, packaging changes) require discussion and
  consensus among maintainers.
* **Breaking changes** or major features should be discussed in
  issues (or
  https://github.com/hyperpolymath/echidnabot/discussions[discussions])
  before implementation.
* **Estate-wide policy questions** (language tiers, CI workflow
  shape, license posture) are escalated to
  https://github.com/hyperpolymath/standards[hyperpolymath/standards].
* **Security issues** follow link:SECURITY.md[SECURITY.md] —
  coordinated disclosure, never via public issues.

== Sunset / Stewardship

If the lead maintainer becomes unavailable for an extended period,
stewardship of echidnabot is to be coordinated by the
gitbot-fleet maintainers collectively. Until that happens, the
project remains under MPL-2.0; any subsequent forks must retain the
same license and attribution per the Palimpsest commentary in
link:PALIMPSEST.adoc[PALIMPSEST.adoc].
